Hi, I'm a self-taught developer.

A little bit about me.

My interest in tech began as a teenager in a small town located in the heartland of Wisconsin. My mother brought home a Macintosh II circa 1988 that was connected via dial-up. I was hooked, seduced by the wide world of the web. I often stumbled upon the source code of various websites and wondered how all that giberish could create the website I was viewing. Never did It think that I could also learn the language of web.

That curiosity then opened a door that could never be shut. Growing up in a household that placed value on creativity and the arts, led me to spend hours exploring adobe photoshop and adobe illustrator. Now, I have found myself at an apex where my desire to merge art and technology has led me to a career in web development. I pride myself on being a life long learner. I am an architecht, a designer, an artist and a writer. My code is clean and elegant. I am a full-stack developer.

Hand-Coded HTML

I focus on writing clean, well-formatted, semantic HTML5 by hand to make sure that the content is easy to read, to collaborate, trouble-shoot and accessible.

Well-Organized CSS

I pride myself on writing CSS that is easy to read and build on. I focus on keeping my CSS lean and fast to load, and I make it a habit to stay up to date on current best practices.
